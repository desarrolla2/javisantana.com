FLATOUT test

This is a visualization that shows the race car path synchronized with a video recorded on track. This is a part of flatout tests, an application to track and measure times for race cars. You can watch a the video in high resolution to see details of how the application works (the android tablet is placed just on the right)

The visualization is done with d3 visualization library, track data is from OpenStreetMap, the video was recorded with a GoPro, it was programmed by @javisantana

You need Flash player 8+ and JavaScript enabled to view this video.
